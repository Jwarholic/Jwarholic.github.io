A little about me:

Fitness is one of my favorite hobbies. One of my life missions is to be the best me and to achieve dreams and goals that will push me into becoming a better version of myself.

I have had many ups and downs with fitness. Through trial and error I have learned what it takes to become in better shape. I have points in my life where I go All in with what I am doing.

I have learned that consitancy is the most important factor in becoming better. Unfortunately, for bodybuilding when I take breaks it shows. I have been down to that 6-9 percent body fat at my peak. Unfourtntely I quickly lose that when I decide I want to eat whatever I want and not exercise or take breaks. Even though I know what I need to do to maintain that dream physique, it's very easy to lose it as well.

My list of goals:

  • Stay in great shape.
  • Learn something new every day
  • Become a web developer/programmer
  • Keep motivated
  • Live life to the fullest
  • Push myself to new limits and experiences
