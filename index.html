About Me:

Fitness is one of my favorite hobbies. One of my life missions is to be the best me and to achieve dreams and goals that will push me into becoming a better version of myself.

I have had many ups and downs with fitness. Through trial and error I have learned what it takes to become in better shape. I have points in my life where I go All in with what I am doing.

I have learned that being consistent is the most important factor in becoming better. How much effort I put in how much I am rewarded. I have been down to that 6-9 percent body fat at my peak. I quickly lose that when I decide I want to eat whatever I want and not exercise or take breaks. Even though I know what I need to do to maintain that dream physique, it's very easy to lose it as well. I'm now more inspired than ever and ready to push myself to new limits.

Goals:

  • Stay in great shape.
  • Learn something new every day
  • Become a web developer/programmer
  • Keep motivated
  • Live life to the fullest
  • Push myself to new limits and experiences
